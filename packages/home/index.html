What are the applications used for?
Caregiver The caregiver application is the starting point of the process. Here you can create, edit and view orders. A list of related patients is provided for each caregiver in a table.
Doctor The doctor application is used to edit and prescribe orders, that were sent to them. If some order doesn't meet their requirement, they can also decline it with a reason.
Pharmacy The pharmacy application provides the final step in the order, giving out the medication. A pharmacist can mark a order item as completed as soon as the medication was given out to the caregiver.
